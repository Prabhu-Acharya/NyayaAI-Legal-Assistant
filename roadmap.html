Suggested Changes Based on Comparison

🔴 URGENT
Add RAG / Vector Search
Biggest gap vs competitor. Pure LLM hallucinates Indian law. Add Pinecone + embed Constitution + BNS acts. Even 500 documents = massive credibility boost. This alone separates a toy from a real legal product.
🔴 URGENT
Redesign Login + Register Pages
Currently plain HTML — looks unfinished vs competitor's glassmorphism UI. First impression = everything. Redesign Register page to match the dark gold aesthetic of ContractGenerator. 1-2 hour task, massive impact.
🔵 HIGH VALUE
Move Profile to Week 5 (Now)
Users need to see their plan status, usage, and logout option. Without Profile page, premium users can't verify they're on Pro plan. Simple to build — Profile.jsx + GET /api/users/me endpoint.
🔵 HIGH VALUE
Add Voice Input (Week 7)
Competitor has it. For Indian users — especially non-English speakers — voice in Hindi/Hinglish is a massive UX win. Web Speech API is free and works in Chrome. 1 day of work.
🟣 MOAT
Build Landing Page Before Week 11
Competitor has one. Without a landing page, the app opens to Register — no context for new users. A simple hero + features + pricing page = credibility, SEO, and conversion. Move to Week 6.
🟣 MOAT
Upgrade Render to Paid ($7/mo)
Both apps use free hosting that sleeps. First request takes 30–60 seconds. This kills conversion. $7/mo on Render = always-on backend. Before showing to any real user or investor, this is non-negotiable.

Indian Legal Framework

ACT / LAWYEARAPPLIED IN
Indian Contract Act1872All contracts — offer, acceptance, consideration
Information Technology Act2000Digital contracts, e-signatures, SaaS agreements
Specific Relief Act1963Contract enforcement & remedies
Indian Stamp Act1899Stamp duty notices on all contracts
Industrial Disputes Act1947Employment contracts, termination clauses
Copyright Act1957IP clauses in NDA & SaaS agreements
Arbitration & Conciliation Act1996Dispute resolution clause in all contracts
DPDP Act (Data Protection)2023Privacy clauses in SaaS & NDA agreements
Income Tax Act (TDS 194J)1961Freelance / Service agreements
Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS)2023Replaces IPC — criminal law citations
